About me

Welcome to my portfolio website! Call me Jhonas, and I'm a full-stack web developer and an aspiring UX designer with a passion for creating projects that live on the internet. Upskilling with the latest tech skills and currently taking a professional certification on UX design by Google, I'm here to help bring your creative ideas to life.

My journey in web development began back in January 2021 when I first started coding with C++ programming. As I explored the technology further, I began building projects and eventually delved into front-end and back-end development.
